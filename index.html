Platforms

OutReach

Student Support and Engagement:

A common source of frustration among students is the lack of coordination and communication among the various college departments that provide information and services. Combined with the out-of-date, unclear, or incorrect information often provided by these offices, this situation results in the waste of students’ valuable time. Having to deal with the many offices responsible for different aspects of college operations and services is intimidating and confusing for most students. OutReach provides colleges with the opportunity to engage and build relationships with their students.  OutReach provides an engaging and empowering environment to direct, focus and nurture students, as well as to connect them to the college community, helping them navigate and feel at home in the college environment. OutReach will help build relationships with students to improve the student experience, reduce dropout rates, and improve organizational efficiency. With OutReach, colleges will have real-time information concerning student service involvement, data, and reports that are useful in managing student service programs. Having knowledge is one thing, but OutReach also allows you to contact students through easy to use communication tools. These tools provide staff member’s immediate access to individual or group student email and phone records. Furthermore, all contact is automatically recorded in a permanent, individualized student record, dramatically reducing staff workload. OutReach features the ability to create automatic campaign notices with follow up and reporting done automatically. OutReach also has built in alerts, which will ensure sure you never forget to respond or follow up on an inquiry. Again, all this communication becomes part of the students' record

With OutReach, a college can:

Target support to underperforming & historically underrepresented students..

Improve student services program communications and target qualified students more efficiently.

Promote collaboration among instructional and counseling faculty for targeted groups.

Supports federal and state initiatives for grants and other funding (CAL SB1456, SB860)

OnRamp

College and Career Readiness (Matriculation Pathway):

The OnRamp module offers a seamless, engaging environment that guides students through the college on-boarding process. OnRamp strengthens support and simplifies the transition of students to higher education by guiding them through the required steps in advance of their actual enrollment. By providing a concise, directed path, OnRamp defines an easy-to-follow pathway to serve a greater number of students transitioning from high school to college. By organizing the requirements and providing a defined path, OnRamp will dramatically increase the chance of a successful matriculation. OnRamp also eliminates many repetitive tasks, helps colleges meaningfully engage students at appropriate times, and provides deep insights into the pipeline of prospects. The analytics of OnRamp will allow colleges to identify enrollment trends, forecast student populations, and develop strategic action plans for even greater institutional efficiencies, satisfying many state legislative mandates.

With OnRamp, a college can:

Provide a structured on-boarding pathway, guiding a student’s matriculation process, increasing college readiness.

Strengthen support for new students and track their individual status in relation to matriculation requirements.

Increase and improve communications and efficiency with feeder High Schools, where the college is in-charge.

Track and report student success scorecards on core California Student Success and Equity Program Initiatives.

Explore

College and Career Readiness (Matriculation Pathway):

Pathways includes an innovative module called Explore. Fundamentally, this module sounds boring and literally does exactly what it says. However, the data knowledge it provides is nothing short of revolutionary. Explore collect data that enables colleges to monitor student progress and the effectiveness of various student services, and target intervention strategies for their students.  The true power of Explore can be seen when you combine demographics with populations and cross reference a specific student service program. This lets the college administrator, councilor or other official get real-time information in relation to participation and status literally within a few mouse clicks. When combined with the OutReach module you now have a powerful tool to target a specific student or number of students who may be qualified for a service, but may not realize it. In addition, Explore can save and prepare user specified, customized reports for state and federal compliance reporting or any internal institutional requirements or meetings. An updated report, once saved, will be no more than a mouse click away.

With Explore, a college can:

Provide trackable and measurable metrics and reports for student service program outcomes.

Determined student trends and extrapolate future needs within student service areas.

Utilize demographic information to increase student involvement.

Pathways (Avail Q2 2017)

Structured Academic Pathways Designed for On-Time Graduation :

The Pathways module completes and connects a user’s experience by offering the learner a clear roadmap to the skills and credentials they need to secure good jobs in the future. Learners are introduced into unique administrative or academic pathways, matched to their career plan, where they will discover and learn, and connect with mentors and other resources to guide them along their paths. Pathways can help people of all ages pursue and attain the education, training, and credentials necessary for skilled jobs in today’s high-growth industries and occupations. Pathways provide transparency for Parents, Educators, and Institutions with real-time knowledge concerning a student’s progress; if they are on-track to attain their educational goal. Pathways not only provides colleges with a platform to promote and deliver to students more efficient pathways to academic completion, but also the tools to manage and deliver commercially developed or school developed career pathways to create stronger alignment between the colleges and the needs of regional economies. With Pathways, colleges can more effectively monitor student progress toward completion through an integrated ‘Proactive Alerting’ system built around guided pathways. This allows student support to be targeted and customized to meet the needs of individual students. As a result, academic advisors can focus their attention almost exclusively on students most in need of services instead of spreading themselves over burdensome caseloads of all students.

With Pathways, a college can:

Create academic pathways, aligned with college requirements for career placement or advancement.

Redesign college offerings that greatly simplify students’ decisions, creating more highly structured programs.

Guide students to stay focused on timely completion of academic goals.

Identify disengaged students through analytics, leading to successful interventions.
